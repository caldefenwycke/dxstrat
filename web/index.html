Mine Bitcoin with Darwin X

A minimal, fast pool UI. Point your miner and go.

Stratum endpoint

stratum+tcp://pool.dxstrat.com:3333

Miner settings

Use your Bitcoin address as the username. Worker name optional.

URL     stratum+tcp://pool.dxstrat.com:3333
USER    bc1qYOURADDRESS.worker
PASS    x

Works with most ASIC dashboards (Bitmain, Braiins, etc.).

Tips

  • Use a worker label to identify each miner.
  • Check your miner time is correct (NTP) for stable shares.
  • Firewall: outbound TCP 3333.

FAQ

What do I use for username/password?
Username is your Bitcoin address. Password can be anything (e.g. x).

When do I get paid?
Once your balance reaches the minimum, we pay out during the daily cycle (~02:00 UTC).
